AcBuy vs KakoBuy — Honest Comparison for 2026

Both AcBuy and KakoBuy are legitimate purchasing agents used extensively by the rep community. They access the same pool of Weidian sellers, which means the products available are largely overlapping. The differences are in community resources, interface, and which categories have the most developed seller ecosystems on each platform.

Community Resources

KakoBuy has the larger community around it. More tutorials, more spreadsheet resources, more QC photos being shared in real time. For a first-time buyer who needs hand-holding through the process, KakoBuy's community infrastructure is an advantage.

AcBuy has a more focused community, particularly around accessories. The AcBuy seller ecosystem for bags, belts, scarves and luxury accessories is better documented than KakoBuy's. If accessories are your primary focus, AcBuy is worth prioritizing.

Interface and QC Process

Both platforms offer QC photos before shipping approval. AcBuy's QC interface has been noted as slightly more organized by buyers who use both — photos are grouped clearly by item and easy to review. KakoBuy's system is functional but slightly more manual in how you request and view photos.

Best Categories by Platform

**KakoBuy is stronger for:** Sneakers (widest seller variety), streetwear basics, Stone Island, Ralph Lauren. Community spreadsheet at replicafind.com/kakobuy-spreadsheet is the most developed of any agent.

**AcBuy is stronger for:** Designer accessories, luxury bags, Gucci/LV/Amiri items, Moncler outerwear. Seller documentation for accessories is most developed on AcBuy. Spreadsheet at replicafind.com/acbuy-spreadsheet.

The Practical Answer

Most experienced buyers use both. Start with KakoBuy for sneakers and streetwear basics, use AcBuy when ordering accessories or luxury items where the community documentation is stronger. Register with invite code XVD384 on AcBuy for member pricing.
